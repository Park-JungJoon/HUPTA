Human Pan Transcriptome Atlas Profile

About Our Web App

HUPTA profile offers a comprehensive Human Pan Transcriptome Atlas derived from a compilation of 46,813 human RNA-seq samples sourced from GEO, GTEx, and TCGA databases.HUPTA profile enables users to explore tissue specificity, housekeeping genes, and consecutively expressed genes. Scores for housekeeping and tissue-specific genes are provided, and researchers can seamlessly access and download data for around 30 different tissues. Our user-friendly interface empowers researchers to effortlessly investigate gene profiles and tissue-specific genes, eliminating the need for coding expertise.
